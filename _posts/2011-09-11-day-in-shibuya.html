--- layout: post title: Day in Shibuya tags: - Japan - optimism - parade - photo - Photo - yoyogi status: publish type: post published: true meta: _wpas_skip_fb: '1' --- I spent Saturday walking around Shibuya, trying to get some decent pictures. Nothing really came out particularly well, but I came across a parade of students. A pretty interesting parade, they were trying to "make Japan happy". I followed them all the way to Yoyogi park, where I talked to them for a while. Turns out there were quite a few people from Keio, so I got some of their numbers, and since a most of them are at Mita campus as well I might meet up with them for lunch sometime.
